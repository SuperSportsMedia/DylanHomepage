Dylan started his basketball career off with Jr. Jaguars from San Antonio for one season then moved on to South Texas Tribe out of San Antonio. There he played year round basketball from 4th grade through 2019. Dylan has always been the leader or one of the leaders of his teams. He studies the game day in and day out and wants to learn from and be coached by the best to keep pushing him further.

In 2019 Dylan Joined Nike ProSkills San Antonio!! At that point he started playing for their national travel team for the 2026 team. This was the first time Dylan wasn’t playing up an age group or with older kids grade wise.

Dylan currently plays for San Antonio Johnson High School. He’s on their Junior Varsity Squad as their starting combo guard. Here he’s averaging 12pts, 4 assists, 3 steals and 4 blocks a game.
